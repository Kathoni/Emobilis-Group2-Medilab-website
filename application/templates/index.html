Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides specialized care for heart conditions.

We treat a wide range of heart diseases with advanced diagnostics and treatments, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Neurology

Specialized care for neurological conditions that affect the brain, spine, and nervous system.

Our team of neurologists provides comprehensive care for conditions like stroke, epilepsy, and neurological disorders.

Hepatology

Our hepatology department offers specialized treatment for liver diseases.

We treat conditions like hepatitis, cirrhosis, and fatty liver disease with state-of-the-art therapies and expert care.

Pediatrics

Comprehensive healthcare services for children from birth to adolescence.

Our pediatricians offer expert care for children, addressing both acute and chronic conditions in a compassionate environment.

Eye Care

State-of-the-art care for all vision-related concerns.
